Chicken with Green Sauce

Mostly unremarkable, but the sauce was good
40m chicken chili pepper tomatillo forgettable

From https://www.seriouseats.com/recipes/2007/09/unclogged-mario-batali-recipe-chicken-with-green-sauce.html

Ingredients

  • 2 lbs. chicken drumsticks & thighs ‚Äì skin removed
  • 3 lbs. husked tomatillos
  • 6 cloves garlic
  • 2 serrano chiles
  • 1 bunch cilantro
  • Handful of scallions, chopped

Process

  1. Place tomatillos, garlic, and serranos in boiling water for 2 minutes, drain
  2. While warm, blend with the juice and zest of 3 limes and 1 bunch cilantro, season generously with salt
  3. Braise chicken in sauce for 25 minutes
  4. Finish with juice of 1 lime and garnish with handful of chopped scallions
